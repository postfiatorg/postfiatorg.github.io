About Post Fiat

Post Fiat is a Decentralized Autonomous Organization dedicated to professional crypto investing in liquid crypto markets

We run this investment DAO to combine the collective judgment of a group of professional hedge fund traders

Unlike Venture DAOs -- Post Fiat aims to generate a high sharpe ratio PNL. denominated in USD.

We use smart contract logic to allocate capital across our trading team. This logic combines quantitative metrics as well as voting of the group in the event a member hits a drawdown limit.
