About Post Fiat

Post Fiat is the decentralized application layer for the post-government epoch.

Level 2 scaling of the Lightning Network combined with the KYC/banking/broker functionality of Square and the Social Layer of Twitter will enable many use cases such as Dapps, in-game currency and NFTs to be built directly on top of the Bitcoin Network.

Post Fiat Org aims to become an early adopter Lightning Node operator that also builds delightful custom software for developers to integrate with.

Post Fiat's team members have worked at top technology companies such as Unity and Palantir. We will be participating at the El Salvador Lightning Hackathon in November
