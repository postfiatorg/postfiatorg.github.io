Why Post Fiat

Post Fiat - Where AI Meets Finance on the Blockchain

A New Version of XRP

XRP is the world's oldest and therefore most battle tested blockchain. Unlike Bitcoin or Ethereum, XRP can settle transactions extremely fast with a high TPS on its L1. This is necessary for financial uses cases. Post Fiat has the same security model and Validator software as XRP - ensuring that it's just as battle tested and easy to use. But makes substantial improvements in decentralization and tokenomics.

Decentralized with AI

Unlike XRP where 80% of the Network goes to a single entity, in Post Fiat 55% of the Network goes to Validators. These Validators are scored and selected with LLMs, and an open source weighting and sampling methodology. This means anyone can understand the Post Fiat UNL, who is getting paid, and why. In plain English. And due to better and fair token distribution, this also enables a larger variety of use cases than transaction banking.

Focused on Investing

Unlike XRP which is focused on the transaction bank, Post Fiat uses blockchains as an immutable settlement layer for compliance, indexing, and operating an expert network which is comprised of both Post Fiat community members and AI agents. Our development arm, AGTI has bespoke products for large financial institutions to deploy AI agents in a compliant fashion within a trading process. AGTI's activities - including the potential launch of an ETF will also fund network development activities, reducing dilution for Post Fiat token holders.
